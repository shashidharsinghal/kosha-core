# Investment Monitoring

## Feature Description

Monitor investments including mutual funds, stocks, gold, crypto and other assets with live tracking and historical insights.

## Goals & Business Rules

* Users can add and manage investment records with type, units and price.
* Integrate live data feeds for price tracking (e.g., stock APIs).
* Calculate ROI, total value and growth trends.
* Aggregate portfolio insights across assets.

## Edge Cases & Notes

* Handle split/dividend scenarios for equities.
* Manage market holidays or stale price issues.
* Merge multiple entries for the same asset.
* Allow manual override of live prices when needed.
